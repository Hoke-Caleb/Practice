Caleb Hoke's Portfolio

In this website, you will be given access to work created during my time in the University of Missouri Learning Design and Development graduate program.The site will cover the core competencies learned throughout the program and include examples from both my classes and the items I created for use in my classroom at Hazelwood East High School.

My graduate program began in August of 2017. During my study I began with two goals: master the principles and nuances of the instructional design field and learn to code for website development. My time at the University of Missouri has helped me to achieve both of these goals. I was also able to further my own knowledge of research and the process of evaluation.

This website is the culmination of these skills. It will provide an overview of what I have achieved during my time in the program and also show the talents of those who have helped me along the way. I have been able to work with numerous talented individuals during this time and would like to thank them for helping me achieve my goals.
